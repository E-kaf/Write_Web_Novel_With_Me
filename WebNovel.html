The Web-Novel

Working Title

The One Without Self

Genre

Epic fantasy with elements of mystery, unique magic system, expansive world-building, and character development.

Core Premise

  • In a world filled with diverse personalities, philosophies, goals and powers, one walks alone, stripped of his identity.
  • There he exists with vast knowledge without an ego that gave context to it.
  • Will he consume his time in this world in pursuit of his past, or will he embrace the present and build towards his future?

What Makes This Story Special

  • Built live with your help — every major plot point, character decision, and world detail can be shaped by chat suggestions.
  • No fixed outline, aside from beginning and an end- the story evolves organically, sometimes in surprising directions.
  • Real-time writing: mistakes, rewrites, and "oops" moments are all part of the experience.
