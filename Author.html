What you need to know about Ekaf Eman

Welcome, I’m Ekaf Eman — an amateur writer with a passion for storytelling and the mastermind behind this live webnovel stream.

As a highly visual learner, I was never truly satisfied with abstract tips or theoretical advice given to me, which is what motivated me to organize this stream: Writing in real time, with all of you, so we can gain some hands on experience together and overall have fun while doing so.

Whether you're here to learn, contribute, or simply enjoy the journey, welcome. Let’s craft something real — one chapter at a time.
